How To Claim Your Prize


Hi guys! Im here to guide you how to claim your PCSO Lotto prizes. But first; Congratulations!
I guess that you won, because you're here reading. If not, I wish you goodluck!

Without further do... lets start

First, write your name together with signature at the back of your winning lotto tickets.
Make sure everthing is correct before claiming.

If you win amounting ₱20 to ₱5,000 pesos, you can direct to any authorized Lotto outlet or at the PCSO branch offices near you.

How about prize amounting more than ₱5,000 pesos; you asked? I have answer for that...

You should go to the PCSO main office at 2nd floor Conservatory Building, 605 Shaw Boulevard, Mandaluyong city.,
for every prize amounting ₱5,000 to ₱200,000 pesos.

And if you are the luckiest man on earth won the JACKPOT PRIZE, you should direct to PCSO main office; the address stated above.
Prizes can be claim from Mondays to fridays at 8am to 4:30pm bring two valid I.D.

So there! you now know how to claim your PCSO Lotto prizes.
So just keep betting, no one knows... you might win.
